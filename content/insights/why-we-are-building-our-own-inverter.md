Most storage startups outsource their inverters. It's faster, but it's a trap. If your power electronics are generic, your system becomes generic. Worse, you inherit every limitation of hardware built for solar, not volatility.

Flywheels demand a different profile:

- **Fast torque response**
- **Low-latency bidirectional flow**
- **Predictable droop behavior**
- **Direct control over rotor dynamics**

This is why we're designing our own architecture. It's how we unlock the performance envelope that lithium-based storage can't reach.
